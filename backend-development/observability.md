# Observability

- How much can you know about a system's internal state by examining external outputs.

## Metrics
- Numeric representation of data measured over intervals of time.

### Four Golden Signals
- Latency: Time to service a request.
- Traffic: Requests per second.
- Error: Error rate of requests.
- Saturation: Fullness of a service (ex. how full is the disk, how much memory is used)

## Logging
- Immutable, timestamped record of discrete events that happened over time.

## Tracing
- The end-to-end request flow encoded into a representation of a series of causally related events.
