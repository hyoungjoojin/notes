# 휴게소 세우기

> Level: Gold 4

## Problem

Given a line segment of length $L$ and $N$ points along the line, we are going to place $M$ additional points such that
the maximum distance between any two adjacent points is minimized. Find the minimum possible value of this maximum distance.
